Hey, I’m Zak and this is a little blurb about me. I’ve committed to art and graphic design as a hobby because of how much creativity it involves and how much I like letting my mind wander. I like to draw things that are towards the horror side of things, with me liking subjects along the lines of eldritch horror and just creepy things in general. The main reason I create art is because it lets my mind do its own thing without having to worry about restrictions or worrying about what I have to do. Essentially, I make art because it is a cozy and safe space to just let my creativity out without any boundaries.
